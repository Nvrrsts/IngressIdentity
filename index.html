IngressIdentity

IngressIdentity is a browser plugin that adds Ingress player info to a player's Google+ profile, to posts, comments, YouTube comments…

No longer will you wonder what the nickname is of this gentleman, or whether that gal is already level 10.

A small overview of the features of this plugin:

  • Supports Chromium / Google Chrome, Safari and Mozilla Firefox.
  • Show player information on the Google+ website:
    • Adds a full overview of the player data to the 'About' tab on a player's Google+ profile.
    • Shows a player's name, level and a list of the anomalies they went to next to post authors, comment authors, event RSVPs…
    • Shows a player's name next to mentions in text.
    • Shows a small overview of the info in the pop-up that appears when you hover over an agent's name.
  • All data is stored securely on Google Drive.
